Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about the Ethical Hacking Masterclass

Do I need any prior experience to take this course?

No prior experience is required. This course is designed for beginners but also covers advanced topics that will benefit experienced IT professionals. We start with the fundamentals and gradually progress to more complex concepts.

What software do I need for this course?

You'll need a computer with at least 8GB RAM (16GB recommended) and virtualization support. We'll guide you through installing Kali Linux and other essential tools in a virtual environment. All required software is open-source and freely available.

How long will I have access to the course materials?

You'll have lifetime access to all course materials, including future updates. This means you can learn at your own pace and revisit the content whenever you need a refresher.

Will this course help me get certified?

While this course covers many topics included in certification exams like CEH and OSCP, it is not specifically a certification prep course. However, the knowledge and skills you gain will significantly help you in preparing for these certifications.

What kind of support is available during the course?

You'll have access to our dedicated student community where you can ask questions and interact with other students. The instructor regularly participates in discussions and provides guidance. Additionally, we offer technical support for any issues with the course platform.
